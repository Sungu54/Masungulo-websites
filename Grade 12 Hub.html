About Us

Empowering South African learners to achieve academic excellence.

Our Mission

SA Grade 12 Hub was created with a single purpose: to democratise access to quality education resources for every South African Matric student. We believe that every learner deserves the tools to succeed, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our platform curates the best official resources from the Department of Basic Education, Independent Examinations Board, and other trusted educational institutions — all in one convenient, easy-to-navigate space.

What We Offer

Official Past Papers: Direct links to DBE and IEB past exam papers and memoranda from 2015 to 2025, organised by subject and year.

Study Guides: Comprehensive topic summaries, formula sheets, and key concept explanations written by experienced educators.

Exam Tips: Proven strategies for time management, question analysis, and mark maximisation from top-performing students and teachers.

Interactive Tools: Flashcards, exam timers, and progress trackers to make your study sessions more effective.
